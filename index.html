A summary


Twenty summers have I been given life's breath, and I'm wasting it all on making illustrations. No regrets, though. Keeps the mind sharp and the imagination even sharper. Because it's hard: it demands every single second of your day. We have to create an entire movie, only the images are solid frozen. That's what it means to draw a story.
